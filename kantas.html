Welcome to Kantas Expanse!


You are adventurers who have come to this far distant land via portal to seek fame, fortune, and probably more fame and more fortune in the small frontier town of Daring Heights...

The Kantas Expanse is an ongoing series of games, a cross between a campaign and one-shot adventures in style of Ars Ludi’s West Marches Campaign. Here you will continue to adventure in these lands, following up on rumours you hear in the local tavern here in Daring Heights. There are a few things you should know:

  • We run games for Kantas Expanse games every week on Tuesday in Holborn and on Wednesday in Greenwich, but due to the standalone nature of each session you can play as often or as infrequently as you like without issue.

  • If you are new: to sign up your first game simply join any meet up with a tag (Beginner friendly). You will need a level 2 character. Create one beforehand, contact your DM for assistance, or come early to make one! Check out our forum or wiki pages for the feel of the campaign

  • If you have played with us before: sign up to any rumour on our forum and enjoy an adventure that your character would love. Or better – create your own rumour! Our DMs like to tailor the session according to players’ ideas.
